  ├── engine.c            1-→ Initialize subsystems, main loop
  │   ├── renderer.c        2-→ Setup OpenGL, render current scene
  │   │   ├── shader.c          3-→ Compile/link shaders
  │   │   └── camera.c          3-→ View/projection matrices
  │   ├── scene.c           2-→ Manage object list, updates
  │   │   └── object3d.c        3-→ Individual object transforms
  │   ├── input.c           2-→ Process keyboard/mouse
  │   └── geometry.c        2-→ Vertex data definitions
  └── math3d.c            1-→ Pure math (no dependencies)
